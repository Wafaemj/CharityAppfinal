About HeartShare

HeartShare is a global non-profit organization dedicated to creating sustainable solutions for communities in need. Founded in 2010, we've impacted over 2 million lives across 50 countries.

12+

Years of Service

Our Mission & Vision

We believe in a world where everyone has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive. Our mission is to bridge the gap between those who want to help and those who need help.

Compassionate Giving

We ensure 90% of donations go directly to programs that help people in need.

Global Reach
